About Me

Hi, I'm Nika Iremadze. Welcome to my personal homepage! I was born in the country of Georgia and moved to the Bay Area in 1999. I graduated from the University of California, San Diego, in 2018 with a B.S. in Biochemistry and Cell Biology. Since then, I have been building my career at Ultima Genomics, where I continue to grow and contribute to groundbreaking advancements in the field.
